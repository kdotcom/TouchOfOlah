About Us

Who we are: Touch of O’lah is an event planning and management services company that presents a fresh way to create, engage and manage events. A service above and beyond is what we strive to provide to every event and for all clients.

What we are about: From the development of creative concepts of an event to its planning, preparation, execution right through to the packaged end product, we do it all. Each detail is meticulously attended to with care and handled with much importance. Nothing is left to chance; everything is done in accordance to the preference & taste of the client.

What we do: We create an event worth remembering from event conceptualization to execution. We are here to help your idea, concept and dream event a reality, develop it and give it a touch of magic that makes each moment a memory to be cherished for all time.

Our Mission: Service-oriented in how we deal with our clients. Focused, driven and with utmost care is how we execute each event. Constantly challenging ourselves and what we can accomplish for others working as a team for and with our clients.

Our Vision

To become a recognized leading events management firm with concept development and service perfromance excellence.
